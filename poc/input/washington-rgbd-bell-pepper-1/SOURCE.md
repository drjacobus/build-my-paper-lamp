# Washington RGB-D Bell Pepper 1

Source: https://rgbd-dataset.cs.washington.edu/dataset/rgbd-dataset/

Download used: https://rgbd-dataset.cs.washington.edu/dataset/rgbd-dataset_full/bell_pepper_1.tar

Selected after the Washington Coffee Mug test.

Why this dataset:

- Real physical household object.
- Turntable capture with multiple camera heights.
- Includes per-frame segmentation masks.
- Solid, silhouette-driven object without handles, holes, or thin parts.
- Better fit for visual hull than the coffee mug, while still more organic/interesting than a sphere.

License caveat:

The dataset page describes the data as available for non-commercial research and education use. Treat this as a POC/research-only dataset unless commercial rights are clarified.
