# Washington RGB-D Coffee Mug 1

Source: https://rgbd-dataset.cs.washington.edu/dataset/rgbd-dataset/

Download used: https://rgbd-dataset.cs.washington.edu/dataset/rgbd-dataset_full/coffee_mug_1.tar

Selected as the second public benchmark after THU-MVS Dog.

Why this dataset:

- Real physical household object.
- Turntable capture with multiple camera heights.
- Includes per-frame segmentation masks, which is useful for testing a geometry-first plus mask-assisted workflow.
- Tests generalization beyond animal figurines.

License caveat:

The dataset page describes the data as available for non-commercial research and education use. Treat this as a POC/research-only dataset unless commercial rights are clarified.
